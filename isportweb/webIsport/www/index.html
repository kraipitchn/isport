Career

Position: Marketing Communication Manager

Report to: Deputy Managing Director

Summary job purpose: Manage marketing communication jobs for the company

Responsibilities:

  • Work collaboratively with all units to create and manage integrated marketing communication strategies
  • Analyze and evaluate the effectiveness of communication methods, cost and results. Recommends solutions to improve those methods
  • Responsible for the development and execution of marketing communication initiatives including product launch, sales collateral, campaigns, social site presence, company website and application, media planning, PR and corporate event
  • Ensure new communication campaigns and programs are aimed at reaching sales targets
  • Manage brand delivery and consistency across all markets for all product lines
  • Build and manage the corporate communication budget
  • Actively manage marketing communication jobs to produce high quality tasks in time and within budget
  • Collaborate closely with managers in other units

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in communications, journalism, marketing or business related fields
  • Minimum of 10-year experience in marketing communication
  • Minimum of 5-year experience managing a marketing communication team
  • Passionate in sport is preferred
  • Experience successfully building coaching and motivating high performing tam while hold employee accountable
  • Demonstrates creative and critical thinking skills
  • Strong business analytics, relationship management, strategy development, project management, problem solving and change management skill
  • Professional and positive approach, self-motivated, team player, dynamic, creative with the ability to work on own initiative
  • Excellent communicator with ability to influence and persuade across all levels of the organization
  • Demonstrates strong oral and written communication skills including the ability to clearly present recommendations and ideas to summarize complex issues

Position: Sport Commerce Manager

Report to: Deputy Managing Director

Summary job purpose: Manage Sport commerce unit to achieve sales KPI

Responsibilities:

  • Co-work on the consumer insight to identify the preferred products
  • Sourcing and negotiating with brands, suppliers and partners for top quality, price and stock of selling products
  • Develop the category to deliver the target of revenue
  • Manage and supervise the constructing and implementing the suppliers and range plans to meet the requirements ad achieve sales and profit target
  • Manage the promotion plan with weekly, monthly, quarterly plan and annual review
  • Daily monitoring market trend in assigned category
  • Coordinative with channels ad internal departments to ensure efficiency in communication marketing to promote products / new products / promotion campaign and logistic in stock planning

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in business administration, marketing or related fields
  • 5-7 years of working experience in product or category manager
  • Passionate in sport is preferred
  • Skill in product management & development, pricing, sales planning, inventory control and understanding customer
  • Good knowledge of online shopping, media
  • Experienced in supplier management
  • Good command in English both speaking writing
  • Strong analytical, negotiation, communication and problem solving skill
  • Some International sourcing experience and has network of suppliers in assigned categories would be a PLUS
